Shuya Chen is a doctoral researcher at the University of Edinburgh. Her supervisors are Professor Vicky Chondrogianni and Dr. Patrick Sturt. She is working on language development and processing in monolingual and bilingual Mandarin-speaking children. She holds an MSc (Distinction) in Developmental Linguistics from the University of Edinburgh with a dissertation in Anaphora resolution in Mandarin-speaking child heritage speakers. She earned a BA in International Education of Chinese at Shandong University, China.
